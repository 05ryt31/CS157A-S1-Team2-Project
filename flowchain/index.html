Connecting Surplus Food to Those Who Need It

FlowChain helps food donors post surplus listings and recipient organizations claim them for pickup — reducing waste, feeding communities.

How It Works

1 📦

Post Surplus

Donors list surplus food items with details like quantity, category, and expiry date.

2 🔍

Browse Listings

Recipients search open listings by category or location to find what they need.

3 🙋

Submit a Claim

Recipients request the food they need with a single click.

4 ✅

Schedule Pickup

Donor approves the claim and sets a convenient pickup time.
